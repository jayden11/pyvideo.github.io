Thank You

A little over six months ago, serious work began on a new PyVideo. The goal was to carry a torch that was at risk of going out (see Welcome to the new PyVideo). Before PyCon 2016, the new site was a humble skeleton of what it is now. The data -- although a tremendous archive, with over 3,000 videos catalogued -- was half of what is now. As PyCon 2016 hit, a team of 20-plus developers sprinted together to ensure this work was not lost.

Now, half a year later, we have a statically generated site with 6,276 videos and counting. In the past month alone, PyVideo.org has serviced almost 15,000 users in 149 countries and generated 50,000 page views. That's more than three-quarters of all countries on earth, and from when we started collecting stats, we've served pages to 93% of the countries on the planet.
